I am studying AQA A Level Computer Science. This involves one exam and two practical parts, a larger long-term project and a controlled practical assessment.



We haven't started the project yet but a topic will need to be chosen by the end of the year. This needs to have an aim or function to fill which it can be graded against depending on how successfully the program fulfils its intended role. Design and development will take place over the first 3 months of Year 13.



The controlled practical assessment will be done at the end of the course and consists of 3 parts, 2 of which about the theory of programming and the last editing, adding to and debugging a previously provided skeleon code.
