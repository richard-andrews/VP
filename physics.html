Team Pryzm is a Colchester Royal Grammar School company with a strong 15-member team. Pryzm is going to design and sell a mental wellbeing resource for ages 11-18. It will be mainly written by the team however input will come from in-depth online research and charities with greater knowledge of certain topic areas. The resource will come in the form of a booklet and a website, with the booklet being the main product generating income and the website offering additional information and advice for free.
